About Lumifavour

Our Story

Lumifavour was founded with a single idea — to help people feel confident, elegant, and unforgettable through the power of scent. We carefully curate premium perfumes that speak to your essence.

From timeless classics to modern blends, every product is crafted with intention and quality ingredients. Our goal is to elevate everyday moments and create signature scents that linger in memory.

Meet our founder: Lumi — a passionate fragrance connoisseur with a vision to share elegance with the world.
